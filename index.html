Course Reflection

What worked well over the last eight weeks and aided your learning process?

The consistent practice exercises and group discussions worked well. They helped me understand complex concepts through practical application and peer support.

What would you change about the course?

I would add more real-world examples and case studies to connect theory to practice effectively. This would enhance understanding and engagement.

Did you learn what you expected to learn in this course?

Yes, I gained the foundational knowledge I hoped for. Additionally, some topics exceeded my expectations such as basic and advanced CSS techniques.

What concepts were new to you, and what concepts have you already encountered in the past?

New concepts included advanced JavaScript DOM manipulation and CSS animations. Concepts I had encountered before included basic HTML structure and inline CSS.
